Brazilian E-commerce key Insights:
-2018 was a record year for online shopping in Brazil, with the highest total orders, driven by increased consumer confidence and improved logistics.
-Credit cards emerged as the preferred payment method,generating the highest total revenue,indicating a trust in online transactions.
-Bed & Bath products topped the charts,with the highest total orders by product category,reflecting the growing demand for home and personal care items.
RECOMMENDATIONS
-Offer credit card payments as a priority,and consider offering installment plans to increase average order value.
-Increase the variety of Bed & Bath products and consider adding related categories like Home Decor and Kitchenware.
-Invest in user-friendly website and mobile app design,and offer personalized recommendations to increase customer engagement.
-Continuously monitor consumer trends and preferences to identify new opportunities and optimize business strategies.
-Consider expanding into other regions in Brazil and exploring new product categories to increase market share.

This project is a visualization of the cholera outbreak in Stockholm in 1853 which was one of the most devastating epidemics in the city's history, claiming over 3,000 lives. The disease spread rapidly through contaminated water and poor sanitation, highlighting the need for improved public health infrastructure.
Cholera Outbreak in Stockholm, 1853 Key Insights:
-September emerged as the deadliest month, with the highest number of fatalities, highlighting the rapid spread of the disease during this period.
-Adults were disproportionately affected, suffering the highest number of deaths, emphasizing the devastating impact on the working-age population.
-stark gender disparity exists, with Females accounting for a significantly higher number of fatalities compared to Males, underscoring the unique vulnerabilities and challenges faced by women during this outbreak.
RECOMMENDATIONS
-Improve access to clean water and sanitation.
-Enhance public health infrastructure and emergency response planning.
-Develop targeted interventions addressing gender-specific vulnerabilities.
-Implement effective communication strategies to educate the public on disease prevention and management.
-Conduct regular epidemiological surveillance to detect and respond to outbreaks promptly.

This report examines the global trends and patterns of shark attacks, highlighting the most affected regions, times of day, and species involved. By understanding these factors, we can better appreciate the importance of shark conservation and take steps to minimize the risk of encounters gone wrong.
Key Insights:
- 2015 had the highest number of reported shark attacks.
-1900 had the lowest number of reported shark attacks.
-Morning had the highest number of reported shark attacks.
-White sharks attacked more people compared to other species.
RECOMMENDATIONS
-Be aware of your surroundings: When surfing, swimming, or engaging in water activities, pay attention to your environment and watch for sharks.
-Avoid peak shark hours: Since most attacks occur in the morning, consider adjusting your water activities to later in the day.
-Stay in groups: When swimming or surfing, stay with a group of people. Sharks are less likely to attack a group than a solo individual.
-Don't enter the water with open wounds: Sharks can detect blood and other bodily fluids, which may attract them to you.
-Avoid wearing shiny jewelry: Sharks may be attracted to shiny objects, so it's best to avoid wearing jewelry while in the water.

The dashboard provides valuable insights for taxi companies, city officials, and ride-hailing services to enhance their services and operations.
New York City Taxi Trip Key Insights:
-Street hail trips account for the highest total number of trips, indicating the popularity of hailing taxis on the street.
-East Harlem North emerges as the most popular area for pickups and drop-offs, highlighting its significance as a transportation hub.
-Weekends see the highest total number of trips, suggesting increased demand for taxis during leisure time.
-The 3 pm to 4 pm time slot records the highest total number of trips, likely due to post-lunch and pre-rush hour travel.
RECOMMENDATIONS
-Taxi companies can optimize their fleet distribution and allocation during peak hours (3 pm to 4 pm) and weekends.
-City officials can use this data to inform traffic management and infrastructure planning, ensuring smoother travel times.
-Ride-hailing services can adjust their pricing and fleet allocation strategies to capitalize on the high demand during weekends and peak hours.
